About ELShahd

Your trusted partner in agricultural exports

Leading Agricultural Export Company

ELShahd is a modern, trustworthy Egyptian company specializing in the export of premium animal feed products and fresh fruits. With our primary focus on animal feed (80% of our business), we have established ourselves as a reliable supplier to livestock farmers and agricultural businesses worldwide.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for international clients seeking consistent, high-grade agricultural products.
