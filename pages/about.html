About ELShahd

Your trusted partner in premium agricultural exports, connecting Egyptian farms to global markets with quality, reliability, and integrity.

Leading the Future of Agricultural Exports

ELShahd stands at the forefront of Egypt's agricultural export industry, specializing in premium animal feed products and fresh fruits that meet the highest international standards.

Founded with a vision to bridge Egyptian agricultural excellence with global market demands, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for livestock farmers, agricultural businesses, and importers worldwide. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.

With 80% of our focus on animal feed products including straw, dried alfalfa, hay, and chaff, complemented by 20% premium fresh fruits, we offer a comprehensive range of agricultural products that support livestock nutrition and global food systems.
