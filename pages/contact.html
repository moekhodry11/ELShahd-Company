Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our products, services, and processes

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product. For animal feed products like straw and alfalfa, we typically require a minimum of 20 metric tons. For fruits, the minimum is usually one container (approximately 15-20 tons). We can discuss smaller quantities for trial orders.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times depend on the destination and product type. Generally, animal feed products take 2-4 weeks for sea freight delivery to most international ports. Fresh fruits require faster shipping (5-10 days) via air freight or express sea services.

Do you provide quality certificates?

Yes, we provide comprehensive quality certificates including laboratory analysis reports, phytosanitary certificates, origin certificates, and any specific certifications required by your country's import regulations.

What payment terms do you accept?

We accept various payment terms including Letter of Credit (L/C), Telegraphic Transfer (T/T), and other internationally recognized payment methods. Payment terms are typically 30% advance and 70% against shipping documents.

Can you customize packaging?

Absolutely! We offer various packaging options including custom bale sizes, branded packaging, and specific packaging materials to meet your market requirements and preferences.

Do you offer samples?

Yes, we can provide samples for evaluation. Sample costs and shipping are typically borne by the customer, but can be adjusted against future orders. This helps ensure the product meets your specific requirements.
